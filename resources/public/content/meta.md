## About This Site

This site was built out of parentheses and higher-order functions, and then ported to slightly fewer parentheses and slightly less higher-order functions. It's just over 400 lines of relatively readable Python. There's still no database; just some [`markdown`](https://daringfireball.net/projects/markdown/) and [`JSONL`](https://jsonlines.org/), processed and then served up by [`tornado`](https://www.tornadoweb.org/en/stable/).

The audio links on some posts were generated with [`catwalk`](https://github.com/inaimathi/catwalk).
